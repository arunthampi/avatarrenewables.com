Modbus Slave Remote Terminal Unit (RTU)

Modbus Slave RTU is a low-cost industrial grade product using quality components for reliability and accuracy. This will be a cost effective SCADA solution for small to medium scale applications. The design is such that the functions are made straight forward, simple and can easily be configured and maintained by a qualified technician.

This unit is primarily designed for SCADA applications (supervisory control and data acquisition) and not intended for logic and sequencing applications like a PLC. The unit will communicate with any HMI (human machine interface) that supports MODBUS RTU protocol for remote control / monitoring and can also be remotely programmed.

The circuit board is treated for better environmental endurance and is housed in a rugged extruded aluminum enclosure.

This product will be commercially available very soon.
