Smart E-Communicator

This product was primarily developed for Oil & Gas industry and can also be used for other applications. This is a Super Capacitor power back-up unit that can source power for enough time for basic monitoring systems to capture important parameters and then transmit it to remote hosts.

Most competing systems do not have this ability and this costs millions of dollars. The concept was approved by the Oil & Gas ministry in Oman and a patent has also been applied for.

This product will be commercially available very soon.
