Smart Communicator

This product was primarily developed for Oil & Gas industry and can also be used for other applications. This is a power back-up unit that can source power for enough time for the basic monitoring system to capture important parameters and then transmit it to remote host. Most of the current monitoring systems does not have this ability and thus lead to millions of dollars loss.

The unit makes use of super-capacitors to store energy and can withstand over half-a-million charge-discharge cycles when compared to a battery which can last for only about 2000 to 5000 charge-discharge cycles.

Smart Communicator can accept 8 digital inputs and the status is accessible remotely from its isolated Modbus RTU serial communication link.

Another distinct feature is that it connects to the main system with only two-wires unlike other “through” systems with at least 4-wires (2-in and 2-out). It just hangs on parallel to the existing 24VDC industrial power supply system allowing easy hook-up and no intrusion to an existing power system.

Since it does not use storage batteries, theft is unlikely.
