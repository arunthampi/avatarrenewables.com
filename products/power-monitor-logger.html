Indoor Air Quality Monitor (IoT-WiFi)

This is a state of the art product from AVATAR Renewables, designed for a large number of ENERGY MANAGEMENT and AUDIT applications.

This microcontroller based equipment has many in-built functions. The device will measure electrical parameters on a 3 phase 4 wire AC circuit. It is a 3-in-1 system that does POWER-MEASUREMENT, DATA-LOGGING and WIRELESS REMOTE DATA TRANSMISSION.

The system has many distinct and powerful features and can find extensive use in:-

  • LARGE BUILDINGS, OFFICES, APARTMENT COMPLEXES
  • SOLAR ENERGY/GRID SYSTEMS
  • POWER AUDITING (records data with time stamp locally)
  • POWER-ENERGY RESEARCH/ANALYSIS
  • GENERATOR SET MONITORING
  • UTILITY/POWER PLANTS
