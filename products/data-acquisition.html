Data Acquisition Unit

We have developed, a cheap and fully functional, stand-alone Data Acquisition Unit. This unit can log 6 analog inputs (at 1Hz frequency) to a micro SD card, along with its corresponding timestamp.

The analogue input range is 0-5V DC corresponding to 0-1023 raw counts. The advantage is that we can manipulate/scale the raw values, which are in CSV format, using Excel.

This product will be commercially available very soon.
