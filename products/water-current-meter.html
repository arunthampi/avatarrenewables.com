Water Current Meter

Cup-type water current meters available in the market today use one pulse per revolution, from a reed-relay/magnet mechanism.

We have developed a cup-type water current meter with a 24 pole magnet rotor and hall-effect sensor, connected to an amplifier/display/logger unit. This stand-alone unit can display record flow (m/s) at 1s resolution and can store data in microSD card (CSV format with timestamp).

This product is very cost effective compared with other models available in market. The unit also can store max/min readings during any recorded period of time.

This product will be commercially available very soon.
