Smart Communicator

In oil & gas industries it is a known fact that huge deferments are caused by power outages. Studies and statistics have shown that about 30% of losses account due to this issue. Generally Power Failure in a Production Pump / Well is flagged as a Communication Failure, a shortfall in prompt corrective action to restore production.

When there is a power failure, the communication among all associated equipment (eg; RTU, Router, Transciever etc) transferring data also lose power, thereby rendering the setup numb. The remote host server for management information can only see and flag it as a communication failureâ€¦a major shortfall.

Smart E-Powered Communicator (SEC) is an innovative, environment friendly power (using Super Capacitors) sourced data communicator meant to transmit last minute process data to central control rooms, before any major power/signal blackout in remote oil installations. SEC signals help prompt corrective action by maintenance team and considerably reduce outages, enhancing production and revenue.
