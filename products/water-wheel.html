Avatar Water Wheel

This brand new concept which consists of a vertical axis water wheel with flipping paddles; was prototyped and tested in Bath University (Avatar 1) and Newcastle University (Avatar 2).

The novelty of this design is that it is fully submersible and the performance is independent of the direction of flow of water.

From the findings of Avatar 1 & 2 tests, we moved on to Avatar 3, with its innovative new design consisting of a jaw-wedge paddle (patent protected design) to capture kinetic energy from low velocity water currents.

Performance tests for Avatar 3 are in progress and will be launched as pilot micro projects in different sites in 2015.
